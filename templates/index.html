Free Data Sources
🔍 Yelp Business Search Free
Searches Yelp for the business owner's name. Works best for local service businesses (plumbers, roofers, restaurants). Adds ~1–2 sec per company.
🌐 Web Search (DuckDuckGo) Free
Searches the web for news and directories that mention the decision maker by name. Good for finding owners who aren't listed on their own website.
📋 BBB Business Search Free
Looks up verified owner/principal names from the Better Business Bureau. High accuracy when found — BBB verifies during accreditation. US only. Adds ~1–2 sec per company.
🏠 HomeStars.ca Search Free · 🇨🇦 Canada
Searches HomeStars — Canada's #1 home services directory for plumbers, roofers, electricians and other trades. Business profiles often show the owner's name. Best source for Canadian local trades.
📞 YellowPages Canada Free · 🇨🇦 Canada
Searches YellowPages.ca for the business contact or owner name. Better Canadian local coverage than Yelp — especially for trades, contractors, and small service businesses.
🗺️ Google Maps Owner Responses Free
Finds the owner's name from Google Maps review responses — business owners frequently reply to reviews signing with their name ("Hi, I'm John Smith, the owner..."). These responses are indexed and searchable.
💼 LinkedIn via Google Free
Searches LinkedIn personal profiles via DuckDuckGo — no LinkedIn account or API needed. Result titles like "John Smith - Owner at Acme Plumbing" give you the name and title in one shot. Highest-confidence external source for decision maker discovery. Adds ~1–2 sec per company.
🗺️ Google Business Profile Free
Fetches the Google Knowledge Panel for the company via Jina.ai to extract the business owner or contact name. Works well when Google has indexed the business listing. Adds ~2–4 sec per company.
📘 Facebook Business Free
Finds the company's Facebook Business page via DuckDuckGo, then reads the About section for owner/founder mentions. Best for small local businesses that manage their own Facebook page. Adds ~2–3 sec per company.
📰 Press Release Mining Free
Searches PRNewswire, BusinessWire, and GlobeNewswire for executive appointment announcements. Best for mid-market and larger companies. Adds ~1–2 sec per company.
🚀 Crunchbase Free
Searches Crunchbase for the company's founders and C-suite executives. Best for tech, SaaS, and startup companies. Adds ~2–3 sec per company.
📧 Web Email Discovery Free
Searches the web for any email at the company's domain mentioned on third-party sites (e.g. forums, press releases, directories). Replicates Hunter.io's approach in real-time — dramatically improves email pattern detection.
🐙 GitHub Email Search Free
Finds company email addresses committed to public GitHub repos (README files, package.json, etc.). Best for tech companies, agencies, and SaaS businesses.
📝 WHOIS Email Lookup Free
Checks the domain registration record for the registrant's email. When the owner used their work email (e.g. jsmith@acme.com), we extract it as a real pattern example. Works on ~20–30% of domains without privacy protection.
✉️ SMTP Email Verification Free
Connects to the company's actual mail server and confirms whether each email candidate exists — without sending a message. Returns a single verified deliverable address. Adds ~3–8 sec per company. Works on ~70% of domains (catch-all servers like Google Workspace are reported as "unverifiable"). This is how AnyMailFinder works.
JS Rendering (Advanced)
🚀 JS Rendering (Playwright) Free
Launches a real Chromium browser to render JavaScript-heavy sites (React, Vue, Wix, Squarespace). Biggest single improvement — fixes ~40–50% of sites that currently return empty content.
Requires one-time setup in Terminal:
pip3 install playwright && playwright install chromium
Slows processing to ~5–8 sec/company. Leave OFF if not installed.
